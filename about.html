ORIGIN STORY

A New Era of Sovereign Defence

Founded in 2021 by a team of aerospace engineers and artificial intelligence researchers, Stackly was built to address the vulnerability of traditional, slow-moving military hardware.

We believed that defense forces needed fast, smart, and fully modular autonomous units that could operate inside contested airspaces and communications-denied fronts without risking operators' lives. Today, we supply sovereign forces across three continents.
